# Data Retention Policy

## Default retention
- **Account data:** retained for the life of the account.
- **Support tickets/messages:** 24 months.
- **Audit logs:** 12 months.
- **Usage/logs:** 60 days.
- **Backups:** 30 days.
- **Deletion requests:** retain for 24 months for compliance.
